The Root Problem

Farmers don't buy technology—they desperately need capital. Traditional AgTech fails because it asks cash-strapped farmers to pay for tools instead of solving their actual problem: access to working capital.

Asking a farmer earning R14k/year to spend R6k on technology = 44% of their income. This is why 95-98% of smallholders reject AgTech. The industry is asking starving people to buy diet plans.
